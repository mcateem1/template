Formal Research Paper

Executive Summary

This report provides a description of the field of mechanical engineering. The goal is to provide a better understanding of the field of engineering and the importance of the different aspects involved with it. Also, how those aspects are incorporated into the career of engineering. It displays the importance of research in mechanical engineering. Engineers use research to find out ways to improve certain pieces of technology. It also reflects on the importance of communication in the field at a local level and at an international level. Communication is used to discuss the data found in testing and research. Also, it goes into detail about the importance of technology in engineering and the impact it has on the field. After that, it analyzes one scholarly article and one trade publication that relate to certain aspects of the field of engineering. Lastly, this paper analyzes and reviews interviews of a member of the Michigan State University engineering faculty staff and of someone who practices engineering as a career.
